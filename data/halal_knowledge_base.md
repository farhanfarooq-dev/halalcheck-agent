# AI HalalCheck Knowledge Base

## Status Definitions

Halal Certified means an official halal certificate is available and can be verified.

Manufacturer Confirmed Suitable means the manufacturer confirmed that doubtful ingredients are plant-based, microbial, synthetic, or otherwise acceptable. This is not the same as halal certification.

No Concern Found means the ingredient list did not contain known doubtful or not-halal ingredients in the local rules.

Doubtful / Needs Verification means one or more ingredients need source confirmation.

Not Halal means a clearly not-halal ingredient was detected.

Unknown means the available product data is missing or incomplete.

## Common E-Codes

E120 is carmine. It is commonly derived from insects and is treated as not halal in this project.

E441 is gelatin. It needs source confirmation because gelatin can come from animal sources.

E471 means mono- and diglycerides of fatty acids. It can be plant-based or animal-based, so manufacturer confirmation is needed.

E472 is a group of emulsifiers. Some cases may need source confirmation.

## Manufacturer Confirmation

Manufacturer confirmation helps clarify whether an ingredient is plant-based, animal-based, microbial, synthetic, or alcohol-derived.

Manufacturer confirmation is useful decision-support evidence, but it is not the same as a halal certificate.

## Doubtful Ingredients

Gelatin, enzymes, emulsifiers, glycerine, glycerol, rennet, flavouring, aroma, and animal fat often need source confirmation.
